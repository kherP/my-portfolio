Overview

Class management and enrolment portal is an internal portal for fitness instructors, personal trainers and the gymmies. They are allowed to check into classes and manage their classes by using the portal.

Client

International fitness company

My role

Lead frontend developer

Challenges

  • Different role with different use cases. Each role of the portal has different use cases. Therefore, we introduced roles and permissions mechanism to control which view is accessible by which user.
  • Different types of users. This portal is meant for all gym instructors and gymmies. Thus, it is impossible for the company to give training to each user to use the application. The team simplified the application and added guide for new users.

The result

  • Positive feedback from the users. The users can easily manage their classes, make appointments, and book classes.
