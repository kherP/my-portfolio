Overview

Customer service platform is also a portal interrelated to Occupational pension portal. This portal is meant for customer service center to follow up with customers on their changes.

Client

Leading Nordic corporate bank

My role

Lead frontend developer

Challenges

  • Microfrontend. Similar to User roles and management portal, this application is also one of the microfrontends within a shell. We use SMACSS methodology in this project to prevent style leakage.
  • Complexity on displaying details for different case types. Each case type has different details. Thus, we use different template for different case type to ensure all the details displayed are relevant to the user.

The result

  • Microfrontend's style aligns with the shell. The microfrontend embedded nicely within the designated shell.
  • Better user experience for Occupational Pension Portal. The customer service can now easily update the users of Occupational pension portal on the statuses of their orders.
